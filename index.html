Supreme Court Majority Simulation Model

A simple model to simulate Supreme Court majority for the next 100 years.

Assumptions:

  • Simulations are ran 1000 times.
  • Amy Barret is confirmed.
  • Death rate is based on acturial life table.
  • New judge is between 45 to 65 years old.
  • New judge has 70% chance to be male.
  • No appointment on election year if president and senate are opposing parties.
  • If President and Senate has split party preference a judge matching the president's preference will be confirmed.
